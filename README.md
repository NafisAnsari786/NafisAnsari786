# Adventure Game: The Quest for the Lost Artifact

Welcome to the **Quest for the Lost Artifact**! In this text-based adventure game, you'll embark on a journey to find a legendary artifact. Choose wisely, as your decisions will affect the outcome of your quest.

## Start Your Adventure

You find yourself at the entrance of a dark forest. Rumor has it that a lost artifact lies within. What will you do?

1. [Enter the forest](#enter-the-forest)
2. [Turn back](#turn-back)

---

### Enter the Forest

You bravely step into the forest. The path splits into two.

1. [Take the left path](#left-path)
2. [Take the right path](#right-path)

#### Left Path

The path is dark and twisted. Suddenly, a wild beast appears!

1. [Fight the beast](#fight-the-beast)
2. [Run away](#run-away)

##### Fight the Beast

You draw your sword and engage the beast. After a fierce battle, you emerge victorious and find the artifact hidden nearby. **Congratulations, you've completed your quest!**

![Victory](https://via.placeholder.com/400x200.png?text=Victory)

[Restart the Game](#start-your-adventure)

##### Run Away

You decide to run away, but you trip and fall. The beast catches up, and your adventure ends here. **Game Over.**

![Game Over](https://via.placeholder.com/400x200.png?text=Game+Over)

[Restart the Game](#start-your-adventure)

#### Right Path

The path is bright and welcoming. You find a friendly traveler who offers you guidance to the artifact. You follow the directions and successfully retrieve the artifact. **Congratulations, you've completed your quest!**

![Victory](https://via.placeholder.com/400x200.png?text=Victory)

[Restart the Game](#start-your-adventure)

### Turn Back

You decide that the forest is too dangerous and choose to head back home. The quest for the artifact remains unfulfilled. **Game Over.**

![Game Over](https://via.placeholder.com/400x200.png?text=Game+Over)

[Restart the Game](#start-your-adventure)
